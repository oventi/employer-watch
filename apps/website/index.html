EmployerWatch

Helping you avoid work exploitation

What is this about?

EmployerWatch is an initiative to identify companies that exploit workers by underpaying them. We are building a database of accurate information about employers who pay under the minimum wage. Our goal is to help prospective employees and potential consumers avoid exploitative companies.

How will EmployerWatch help people?

We have a number of initiatives, among them:

  • Searchable database over the web: Search employers by name, location and industry and find if they are exploiting workers or have done it in the past.
  • Searchable database app: Similar to the searchable database, but in the form of a mobile app
  • Reporting system: A way to anonymously report employers who exploit,
  • Informative sources: An website, video channel, digital publications and newsletters, social media groups and other sources to get updates and help on fair work.

How can I help EmployerWatch?

Tell your story

If you have even been exploited at work, you can tell your story. You will remain anonymous, but we will ask you if you can name your employer and its location. You will be helping other people avoid this situation in the future.

Organise information

There is a lot of data that needs to be organized. Help us get that information in a better way to make it searchable. No advanced IT knowledge is required, just basic computer and internet skills.

Work on IT

There is a number of advanced tasks to do: programming, data handling, design, content writing, and video making.

Donate money

Fund EmployerWatch and help us pay people fairly to work on our initiatives

Get in touch

Contact us to arrange for you to tell your story, organise information, work on IT, or donate money. Let us know how you can help EmployerWatch.
